Roku’s screensaver was once a flat, overlooked feature despite hundreds of millions of impressions daily. As a self-initiated project, I reimagined the screensaver as an interactive cityscape that blends entertainment seamlessly with ads. The design transformed a passive experience into a powerful ad platform.

I had a lot of fun drawing inspiration from my deep love for entertainment to conceptualize a universe of movie-themed easter eggs to encourage engagement. I personally oversaw the entire process from concept to QA.

The result is part feature and part content that transformed a passive experience into an interactive iconic ad platform that has generated over 100M dollars in new ad revenue.

The Roku screensaver became an defining part of the Roku brand, prominently featured across the Roku campus, industry events, and TV shows. Building on its success, an entire ad platform was developed, integrating content and full thematic takeovers with ads, further driving revenue growth.
