SPARQ Sensory Performance is a system of technologies, products and programs designed to assess, analyze and improve an athlete's visual and sensory performance.

I led the design and development of Nike’s SPARQ Sensory Performance Training OS and its companion mobile app, overseeing the entire process from concept to final production.

The work encompassed interaction design, prototyping, motion design, usability testing, and close collaboration with engineers inside and outside of Nike to ensure a seamless and timely delivery of the training OS.

The system enabled trainers to administer standardized tests, generating actionable performance insights to improve athletes’ visual and motor skills.
